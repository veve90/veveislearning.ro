====== IPTable======

**Good references**
  * [[http://wiki.centos.org/HowTos/Network/IPTables]]

**What is it?** IPTable or iptables/netfilter is a built in firewall of CentOS.

**How to check the iptable rules?**
''iptables -L''

**How to modify the iptable rules?**
You can modify the rules in the file ''/etc/sysconfig/iptables''.
