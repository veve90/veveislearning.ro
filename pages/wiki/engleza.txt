====== Limba Engleza ======


Uneori incep sa uit anumite aspecte din limba engleza, sau incep sa le folosesc prost; asa ca m-am hotarat sa notez ceea ce caut/ uit. 

===== He hasn't spoken vs he did not speak =====
^ He hasn't spoken (Present Perfect)      ^ He did not speak (Past Simple)    ^
| Unfinished action that started in the past and continue to the present.    | Finished action     | 
| Unfinished action that started in the past and has an influence into the present    | Finished action with no influence to the present |
|** this week, just, already, ever, just, never, not yet, so far, till now, up to now**    | **last week, yesterday, 2 minutes ago, in 1990, the other day, last Friday**     |
