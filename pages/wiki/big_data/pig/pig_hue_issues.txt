====== Pig Issues in Hue ======
===== Not completing the Pig parameters in the Hue editor =====

If you want to try the pig parameters in the Hue editor, 
and you are **not** completing the **parameters pop-up**,
you will get an **error for every single query** for that user on that computer! (even a clear table) 

**What you have to do:** clear you browser cache
