====== Cloudera ======
Cloudera is a company that focuses on Apache-Hadoop and that created the **Cloudera distribution of Hadoop**=> CDH.

  * Provides support, consultng,training for Hadoop users
  * Cloudera Staff are commiters to most of the Hadoop projects
